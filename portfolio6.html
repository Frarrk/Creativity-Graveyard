Portfolio 6 – Types of Computers

Overview

Modern computing systems range from compact microcomputers to massive supercomputers. These categories differ in architecture, purpose, and performance. Understanding the hierarchy of computer types helps explain why different industries require different machines customized for their workloads.

Discussion

Supercomputers offer extreme computational power using massive parallelism, enabling tasks like climate modeling. Mainframes and servers prioritize reliability and large-volume transaction processing, serving banks, enterprises, and governments.

Workstations provide high-performance computing for individual professionals in fields like 3D design and engineering. Microcomputers such as PCs, laptops, and smartphones support everyday personal and office tasks with balanced performance and affordability.
